What easli does

From envelope to reply.

Three steps from "what does this mean?" to "done".

📷

Multi-page scanner

Native camera with automatic edge detection, multiple pages in one scan. PDF upload works too.

🌍

25 explanation languages

From French to Hindi: you read the explanation in your native language, the original letter stays in its source language.

⏰

Deadlines detected

easli automatically detects dates and monetary amounts — and can remind you locally on your device.

✉️

Reply draft

A polite, factual reply draft in 32 languages — plus a short explanation in your language so you know exactly what you're sending.

💬

Ask anything

Not sure about a paragraph? Ask easli in English or your own language — instant answer, document-scoped.

⚠️

Risk warning

easli flags urgent or critical letters (reminders, terminations, fines) so you never miss a deadline.

How it works

Done in two minutes.

No account. No profile. Just you and the letter.

1

Photograph the letter

Hold the camera over the letter — easli automatically detects pages and straightens them.

2

Read the explanation

Within seconds you get a calm, clear explanation in your language. No legalese.

3

Send a reply

Pick from suggested reply options. easli writes the text in the sender's language. Open directly in your Mail app and send.
